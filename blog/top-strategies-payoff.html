Top 5 Strategies to Pay Off Your Home Loan Faster

Short intro: Why paying off faster matters — interest savings, peace of mind.

1. Make regular small extra payments

Even small monthly add-ons reduce interest and shorten tenure.

2. Make a lump-sum prepayment when you get a bonus

Large one-time payments have bigger compounding effect on interest saved.

3. Switch to shorter tenure if you can afford higher EMI

Reducing tenure reduces interest drastically.

4. Re-amortize (if your bank allows)

After prepayment, ask your bank to recalculate EMI/tenure.

5. Use the calculator to test scenarios

Run combinations of prepayment + tenure to pick optimal plan.
